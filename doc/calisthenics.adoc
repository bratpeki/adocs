
= List of Calisthenic Exercises
:toc:

Allows easily looking up what exercises hit what muscle group,
so you can make a routine that works best for you and still hits everything.

== Muscles by Group

* Push
** Chest
** Shoulders
** Triceps

* Pull
** Back
*** Traps
*** Lats
*** Lower Back
** Biceps
** Forearms
** Rear Deltoids (Back of the shoulders)

* Legs
** Quads
** Hamstrings
** Glutes
** Calves
** Adductors and Abductors

* Core
** Abs
** Obliques
** Deep Core
** Hip Flexors

* Neck
