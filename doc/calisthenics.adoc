= Calisthenics
:toc:
:toclevels: 2

Calisthenics is a form of training that relies on your own body weight.
It's very easy to do anywhere, even at home, which is why it's my go-to.

Below is a categorized list of exercises and the muscles primarily and secondarily worked.

== Push Exercises

[cols="1,1,1"]
|===
| Exercise | Primary Muscles | Secondary Muscles

| Push-ups
| Chest
| Triceps, shoulders

| Decline Push-ups
| Upper chest
|

| Diamond Push-ups
| Inner chest, triceps
|

| Pike Push-ups
| Shoulders
|

| Tricep Dips
| Triceps
|

| Bench Dips
| Triceps
|
|===
