
= List of Calisthenic Exercises
:toc:

Allows easily looking up what exercises hit what muscle group,
so you can make a routine that works best for you and still hits everything.

== Muscles by Group

* Push
	** Chest
	** Shoulders
	** Triceps

* Pull
	** Back
		*** Traps
		*** Lats
		*** Rhomboids
	** Biceps
	** Rear Delts (technically part of the shoulder, but trained by pulling)
	** Forearms

* Legs
	** Quads
	** Hamstrings
	** Glutes
	** Calves, Tibialis Anterior (opposite each other)
	** Adductors, Abductors (opposite each other)

* Core
	** Abs, Deep Core
	** Obliques
	** Lower Back
	** Hip Flexors

* Misc
	** Neck
